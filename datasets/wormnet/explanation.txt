Dataset Name:
    wormnet
Description: 
   Gene functional associations, where vertices represent the genes.
   Wormnet is a network by integration of all data-type-specific networks by modified Bayesian integration.
   This includes: CE-CX, CE-GN, CE-GT, CE-HT, CE-LC, CE-PG, DM-CX, DM-HT, DM-LC, DR-CX, HS-CX, HS-HT, HS-LC, SC-CC, SC-CX, SC-HT, SC-LC, SC-TS.
