Dataset Name:
    condmat
Description:
    Collaboration network of Arxiv Condensed Matter category.
    There is an edge if authors coauthored at least one paper.
    Each unordered pair of nodes is saved only once.
Type:
    Unweighted, directed
Size:
    23133 nodes, 186936 edges
Citation:
    J. Leskovec, J. Kleinberg and C. Faloutsos. Graph Evolution: Densification and Shrinking Diameters.
    ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data (ACM TKDD), 1(1), 2007.
